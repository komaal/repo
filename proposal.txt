FINAL PROJECT PROPOSAL 
 
INTRODUCTION: 
We propose to implement a project for “Hotel Management System”. During 
summer tourists from all over the world are attracted by fascinating northern areas 
of Pakistan. We intend to implement a website to enable online reservation 
services of our hotel. 
FEATURES: 
Our website provides following features: 
For admin: 
 Login 
 edit and update room status 
 generate form 
For user: 
 Register user 
 Login 
 Check availability for room 
 Reserve room 
 Add personal contact details 
 Edit reservation 
 Delete reservation
